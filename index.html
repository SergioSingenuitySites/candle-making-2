Good to know

Frequently asked

Do I need a reservation?+

Walk-ins are welcome 7 days a week at both locations. We recommend booking on weekends, holidays, or for groups. If your preferred time isn't online, call or text the store.

How long does the candle experience take?+

Most visits last 30–45 minutes; larger groups may take 45–60 minutes. Because everything is customized, you can take your time or pour quickly.

When can I pick up my candle?+

Candles set in about 90 minutes (longer for large vessels). Shop or grab a bite nearby and swing back, or pick up the next day.

What does it cost?+

You only pay for what you make. Most products range from $18–$70; our 8 oz candle is $26 and a guest favorite.

Can kids and dogs join?+

Yes! Kids 8+ do great at the fragrance bar, and younger children are welcome with close supervision. Friendly, leashed dogs are always welcome.

Do you host parties and events?+

Absolutely — birthdays, showers, date nights, and team events. Parties of 8+ receive a free host gift when all guests attend. We also offer weddings, wholesale/white-label, and mobile events.
